## Inferred Brief

Product: Thoroughbreds.ai, an AI-assisted catalogue, stable, breeding, sales, and
racing intelligence platform for thoroughbred professionals.

Primary offer:

- Free iPhone and iPad catalogue scoring app.
- Free My Stable horse CRM.
- Paid upgrade path for AI insights, professional reports, advanced statistics,
  and team workflows.

Primary audiences:

- Bloodstock agents.
- Owners and syndicators.
- Breeders and studs.
- Trainers and serious buyers preparing for sales.
- Veterinarians or advisors who contribute notes and inspection context.

Likely geography: Australia first, with global thoroughbred applicability.

Primary conversion:

- Create free account.
- Download or open the iOS app.
- Upgrade when AI insights, reports, or team collaboration are needed.

Landing pages:

- https://www.thoroughbreds.ai/mobile-app
- https://www.thoroughbreds.ai/
- https://app.thoroughbreds.ai

Brand constraints:

- Premium but practical.
- Data supports judgement; it does not replace an experienced horseperson.
- Avoid claims that AI can identify winners, guarantee black type performance,
  remove risk, or replace veterinary or agent judgement.
- Do not imply affiliation with Inglis, Magic Millions, Arion, Equineline, or
  other sales/data providers unless separately approved.
- Do not use private client horses, catalogue data, sale results, account IDs,
  ad account IDs, or customer lists.

## Pattern Extraction

Repeated buyer tensions:

- Catalogues are too large to handle cleanly with memory, paper, and scattered
  spreadsheets.
- Sale-day decisions are time-compressed, especially after inspection notes or
  team opinions change.
- Professionals want data, but only when it respects the craft of bloodstock
  judgement.
- Free tools are a low-friction way to get serious buyers into the workflow.
- The strongest upgrade story is not "AI replaces you"; it is "AI keeps the
  context organised while you make the call."

Recommended first batch shape:

- 3 problem/solution ads.
- 2 proof or authority ads.
- 2 product/demo ads.
- 1 comparison ad.
- 1 urgency/event ad.
- 1 wildcard learning ad.

## Copy Guardrails

Approved phrasing themes:

- "decision support"
- "better context"
- "score lots faster"
- "build a cleaner shortlist"
- "AI insights when you need them"
- "your judgement stays central"
- "free to start"

Avoid:

- "guaranteed winners"
- "predicts black type"
- "never miss a good horse"
- "replace your agent"
- "beats every competitor"
- "first to market" unless separately substantiated for paid advertising
- implied affiliations with sales companies, data providers, or catalogue owners

## Asset Production Notes

Use real approved product screenshots where available. If unavailable, use
abstract non-readable interfaces that communicate workflow without representing
specific product states.

Required source files before final design:

- Approved Thoroughbreds.ai logo files.
- Approved brand colors and typography.
- Approved iOS screenshots or screen-recording clips.
- Approved horse/sale-yard imagery with model, property, and usage rights.
- Approved App Store badge file.

Reject generated assets that:

- include competitor logos or sale-company marks,
- show readable private lot data,
- make the product look like a betting or tipster service,
- imply guaranteed racing outcomes,
- distort horse anatomy,
- look like generic luxury stock imagery with no product workflow.
